Our Mission

We are an independent nonprofit organization that supports the dedicated shelters and rescue groups of New York City by posting their adoptable pets. Each day, thousands of stray dogs and cats are forced to live in our city's streets and alleyways. Alone, starving, and in desperate need of veterinary care, these poor animals struggle day by day and it is our mission to help them find caring and loving forever homes so that no adoptable pet is euthanized for lack of a place to call home.
